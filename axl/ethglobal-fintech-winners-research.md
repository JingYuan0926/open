# ETHGlobal Hackathon Winning Projects: Fintech & AI Focus (2024-2025)

Research compiled from ETHGlobal hackathon results across 2024-2025. Filtered for projects
that go beyond simple P2P crypto/swaps and address real-world fintech problems.

---

## 1. Kyma Pay
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal New York 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Stablecoin payment processor for merchants with ultra-low 0.15% fees. Supports multiple stablecoins and provides merchant-facing infrastructure for accepting crypto payments seamlessly.
- **Tech Stack:** AMM (Orbital-based), HTTP 402 protocol, Coinbase integration, smart contracts
- **Category:** Cross-border payments infrastructure / Merchant payments

## 2. TX Delay Insurance
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal New York 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** On-chain insurance product against transaction delays on EVM networks. Users purchase insurance for time-sensitive transactions and receive compensation if their transaction is delayed beyond a threshold, with claims verified via on-chain timestamp data.
- **Tech Stack:** RPC proxy, EVM smart contracts, verifiable timestamp oracles
- **Category:** Insurance / Risk management

## 3. Swap Pay
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal New York 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** SDK that simplifies Web3 payments by allowing users to pay using any combination of tokens in their wallet without manual conversion. A single transaction replaces multiple exchanges, approvals, and trades.
- **Tech Stack:** Chainlink data streams, EIP-5792, PYUSD settlement layer
- **Category:** Payments infrastructure

## 4. Noah
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal New York 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Digital asset inheritance solution that automates wealth transfer through smart contracts using a dead-man's-switch mechanism. Solves the real-world problem of crypto estate planning and succession.
- **Tech Stack:** Smart contracts, Uniswap, Fern service, Flow, Chiliz, Katana
- **Category:** Compliance / Estate planning / Financial inclusion

## 5. Pika Vault
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal New York 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Cross-chain asset management platform enabling deposits and withdrawals across multiple blockchains while maintaining rebalanced portfolios. Supports qualified custodial addresses meeting institutional regulatory requirements.
- **Tech Stack:** ERC-4626, ERC-7540, Circle CCTP, LayerZero, Chainlink CCIP, USDC
- **Category:** Cross-border payments / Institutional compliance / Asset management

## 6. x402-flash
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal New York 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Micropayment protocol that reduces payment latency from 200ms to double-digit milliseconds through escrow mechanisms. Enables real-time machine-to-machine and API payments.
- **Tech Stack:** x402 standard, escrow contracts, Circle Paymaster, USDC
- **Category:** Payments infrastructure / API monetization

## 7. Omma Cash
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Brussels 2024 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Enables users unfamiliar with Web3 to send, bridge, and on/off-ramp money entirely through WhatsApp using natural language prompts. All blockchain complexity is abstracted away behind a chat interface.
- **Tech Stack:** Circle API, programmable wallets, Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol (CCTP), USDC, WhatsApp integration
- **Category:** Financial inclusion / Cross-border payments

## 8. Piggy Wallet
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Brussels 2024 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Crypto savings app for children designed to enhance financial resilience in hyperinflationary economies. Features include time-locked savings, automatic USDC exchange, savings goals, task bounties, AI financial advisor, and parental insurance controls.
- **Tech Stack:** Smart contracts, USDC, AI advisor integration, parental control modules
- **Category:** Financial inclusion / AI + finance / Insurance

## 9. Individuum
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Brussels 2024 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Automated marketplace for web tasks that guarantees completion to specification and ensures transparent, secure payments. Uses cryptographic verification (TLS Notary + MPC) to verify task completion before releasing funds.
- **Tech Stack:** TLS Notary, MPC, Arbitrum Stylus smart contracts, Rust backend, Next.js frontend
- **Category:** Compliance / Secure payments / Gig economy finance

## 10. AdFHE
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Brussels 2024 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Privacy-preserving personalized advertising protocol using Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE). Enables ad targeting by computing on encrypted user data without ever decrypting it -- solving the compliance tension between personalization and data privacy (GDPR/etc).
- **Tech Stack:** Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE), smart contracts, encrypted computation
- **Category:** Compliance / Privacy / KYC-adjacent

## 11. AgentForge
- **Hackathon:** Agentic Ethereum (January 2025)
- **What it does:** No-code platform for creating and deploying AI-powered DeFi vaults. Fund creators define custom strategies and policies; AI agents then monitor market conditions, execute yield optimization across chains, and distribute profits according to preset fee structures.
- **Tech Stack:** React/Next.js, Node.js, Python (market analysis), Lit Protocol Agent Wallet, AAVE, Chainlink CCIP, ERC-4626 vault standard, multi-chain (Arbitrum, Base)
- **Category:** AI + finance / Lending / Cross-chain asset management

## 12. YieldMax AI
- **Hackathon:** Agentic Ethereum (January 2025) -- Won Coinbase AgentKit Pool Prize
- **What it does:** AI-powered yield maximizer for stablecoins. The AI agent automatically analyzes DeFi protocols, selects optimal lending strategies, and executes risk-aware investment strategies while users maintain control through dedicated AI-Agent-powered wallets.
- **Tech Stack:** Base, Coinbase Developer Platform (CDP), AgentKit (Coinbase), Autonome Platform, GPT models, smart contracts
- **Category:** AI + finance / Lending / Risk management

## 13. Nimble
- **Hackathon:** Agentic Ethereum (January 2025) -- Finalist + Coinbase AgentKit Pool Prize
- **What it does:** AI-agent-based solver network that helps users get the best execution price for swaps, plus deposits/withdrawals into Morpho lending vaults. Multiple solver agents compete to find optimal routes.
- **Tech Stack:** Coinbase AgentKit, OpenAI API, Node.js, Base (EVM), Morpho vaults
- **Category:** AI + finance / Lending

## 14. MintCondition
- **Hackathon:** ASI Hackathon at ETHGlobal NYC 2025 -- 2nd Prize
- **What it does:** Multi-agent appraisal system using multiple LLMs for independent price evaluation, then applying symbolic reasoning (MeTTa) to reach consensus with confidence-scored price ranges. Demonstrates decentralized, transparent price discovery through agent collaboration.
- **Tech Stack:** Multiple LLMs, MeTTa symbolic reasoning, ASI:One, WebSocket infrastructure, streaming agent logs
- **Category:** AI + finance / Valuation / Risk assessment

## 15. ETHPark-QR
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Bangkok 2024 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Crypto-to-fiat payment bridge allowing users to pay Thai parking bills on-chain using cryptocurrency, without needing a local Thai bank account. Supports both internal direct settlements and third-party payment settlements.
- **Tech Stack:** Blockchain payment layer, QR code integration, fiat settlement rails
- **Category:** Cross-border payments / Financial inclusion

## 16. QuickPay
- **Hackathon:** Superhack 2024 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Enables USDC holders on Base to make gas-free payments at physical merchants in Mongolia through the existing QPay system. Bridges crypto and traditional payment infrastructure without requiring merchants to integrate crypto directly.
- **Tech Stack:** Base (L2), USDC, QPay (Mongolian payment system), gas abstraction
- **Category:** Cross-border payments / Financial inclusion

## 17. Century Pay
- **Hackathon:** Superhack 2024 (Finalist) -- Won Best Payment Application (Base), Best UX/UI in DeFi (Mode)
- **What it does:** Discord bot enabling on-chain payments via simple chat commands. Users connect AA wallets, send ETH, stake, and transact with gas fees covered by a paymaster. Targets communities and DAOs needing frictionless payment flows.
- **Tech Stack:** Discord bot, Account Abstraction wallets, passkey authentication, paymaster (gas sponsorship), Base, Blockscout
- **Category:** Payments infrastructure / Financial inclusion

## 18. PolyBets
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Cannes 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** AI-driven smart routing protocol that aggregates prediction markets to unify liquidity, optimize betting configurations, and maximize returns. Uses Claude AI for intelligent market adaptation and continuously scans for arbitrage opportunities across platforms.
- **Tech Stack:** Claude AI, Oasis Sapphire (privacy chain), Polymarket integration, LMSR pricing, automated market monitoring
- **Category:** AI + finance / Risk management / Insurance-adjacent (prediction markets)

## 19. Beam Pay
- **Hackathon:** ETHGlobal Cannes 2025 (Finalist)
- **What it does:** Payment infrastructure using EIP-7702 to enable ERC-20 token payments with on-chain metadata in a single transaction. Supports multi-chain deployment. Solves the problem of attaching invoicing/compliance data to blockchain payments.
- **Tech Stack:** EIP-7702, browser plugin, smart contracts, Zircuit, multi-chain
- **Category:** Payments infrastructure / Compliance

## 20. Synapse (Honorable Mention)
- **Hackathon:** ASI Hackathon at ETHGlobal NYC 2025 (Honorable Mention)
- **What it does:** Cross-chain investment co-pilot -- an AI-powered DeFi navigator that unifies fragmented wallets, protocols, and chains into a single intelligent interface. Analyzes risks, maximizes yield, and executes safe transactions across chains with one click.
- **Tech Stack:** The Graph, SingularityNET ASI models, Circle wallets, Coinbase SDK, LI.FI/LayerZero bridges
- **Category:** AI + finance / Risk management / Cross-chain

## Key Trends Observed

1. **AI Agents as Financial Advisors**: Multiple winners use AI agents (built on frameworks like Coinbase AgentKit, Fetch.ai uAgents) to automate DeFi yield optimization, risk assessment, and portfolio management.

2. **Payments Abstraction**: Strong trend toward hiding blockchain complexity -- users interact via WhatsApp, Discord, voice commands, or browser extensions while crypto settlement happens invisibly.

3. **Cross-Chain as Default**: Nearly all payment/finance projects support multi-chain operations using bridges like LayerZero, Chainlink CCIP, and Circle CCTP.

4. **Real-World Fiat Bridges**: Projects like QuickPay (Mongolia) and ETHPark-QR (Thailand) demonstrate practical crypto-to-fiat payment bridges for specific local economies.

5. **Privacy-Preserving Compliance**: FHE, ZK proofs, and TLS Notary are being used to solve the tension between regulatory compliance and user privacy.

6. **Stablecoin-First Architecture**: USDC dominates as the settlement layer across payment and lending projects, with PYUSD emerging as an alternative.
